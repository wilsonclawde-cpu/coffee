Our place

A proper coffee shop, run by people who care.

Kesteven Coffee Shop sits at the heart of the Kesteven community in Sleaford. We pull speciality espresso, brew filter worth lingering over, and make our food fresh on site — nothing flown in frozen.

Whether you're catching up with a friend, getting heads-down with a laptop, or just stealing ten quiet minutes, there's a seat here with your name on it.
